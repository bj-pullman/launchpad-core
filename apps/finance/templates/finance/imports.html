What should I import?

Use this quick guide if you have not worked in Finance imports recently.

Records

Use records for tracked finance items such as renewals, purchases, subscriptions, service contracts, assets, and operational expenses that should remain visible in the Finance module.

Transactions

Use transactions for raw ERP, accounting, purchasing, or ledger activity rows imported for review, reconciliation, reporting, or later promotion into tracked finance records.

Vendors

Use vendors to create or update supplier, provider, reseller, or contractor contact information including websites, phone numbers, billing contacts, support contacts, and sales representatives.
