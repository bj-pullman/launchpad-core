Import Workflow

Finance now uses a ledger-first model. ERP audit trail files should be imported as Ledger Transactions, then linked to Records, Budget Accounts, and Purchase Orders.

Ledger Transactions

Use this for eFinance Expenditure Audit Trail exports and other raw ERP activity. These rows become the accounting source of truth for Budget Accounts, Purchase Orders, spending, encumbrances, and Record matching.

Managed Records

Use this only for curated Finance items you already manage outside the ERP, such as known renewals, subscriptions, service contracts, and manually tracked operational items.

Vendors

Use vendors to create or update supplier, provider, reseller, or contractor contact information.

Upload

Start a manual import. For ERP exports, choose Ledger Transactions.
